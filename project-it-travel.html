IT Travel Request

Redesigning organizational workflows for global IT travel logistics.

The Challenge & Business Goals

Optimizing the Enterprise Operational Loop

As the Design Manager, I overhauled the organization's legacy travel request system, transforming a bureaucratic hurdle into a streamlined, policy-intelligent workflow. By automating 60% of manual approvals, we reduced the average booking time from days to minutes, significantly improving operational readiness for global IT teams.

Key Result

65% Faster

Reduction in average end-to-end booking time, from initial request to final finance approval.

Client

Global Tech Corp

Role

Design Manager

Timeline

4 Months

Platform

Internal Web Tool

01. Discovery & Research

Mapping the Bureaucratic Journey

The core issue wasn't the UI—it was the decision architecture. I mapped the 14-step approval chain and identified that most stalls happened because of policy ambiguity. My strategy was to embed the "policy engine" directly into the form logic, providing real-time feedback and automating "safe" approvals based on employee seniority and budget status.

Workflow Synthesis

Consolidated 3 disparate legacy systems into a single "Source of Truth," eliminating data duplication and manual entry errors.

Policy Automation

Designed a rule-based engine that automatically verified requests against global travel policies, reducing manual HR audits by 80%.

Feedback Loops

Implemented a "live-status" tracker for employees and approvers, creating transparency and ending the need for follow-up emails.

02. Strategy & Architecture

Streamlining the Journey

Form Simplification

The core design challenge was turning a massive bureaucratic form into an intuitive wizard. We implemented progressive disclosure, showing only relevant fields based on the trip's complexity. Auto-filling employee data and historical preferences eliminated repetitive data entry.

Actionable Dashboards

For managers and finance approvers, we designed a dense but highly scannable dashboard. By utilizing neon accents (green for under budget, red for policy violations), approvers could evaluate and greenlight requests directly from the home screen in seconds.

03. Execution & Design System

The Challenge

Enterprise IT employees were spending hours navigating a complex, multi-tiered travel request system. Forms were redundant, policies were opaque, and approval chains often stalled for days.

The Solution

We redesigned the enterprise booking application with a focus on intuitive forms, automated policy checks, and an overarching dark mode aesthetic. By simplifying the UI and integrating real-time budget views, we reduced processing time and improved manager approval speed.

04. Final Solution & Impact

Frictionless Operations

The rebuilt travel application drastically reduced the administrative burden on both employees and management, saving the enterprise thousands of hours annually and ensuring near-perfect policy compliance.
