Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services do you offer?
I specialize in end-to-end product design, UX strategy, design system architecture, and leading teams to deliver complex enterprise and consumer applications.
2. How does the design process work?
My process follows a structured methodology: Discovery & Research, Strategy, Conceptual Design, Iterative Prototyping, and Final Delivery/Handoff.
3. How long does a project usually take?
Timelines vary by scope. A typical MVP design phase takes 4-8 weeks, while large-scale enterprise systems involve long-term partnership.
4. What do I need to provide before starting?
A clear business goal, understanding of your target users, and any existing technical constraints or brand guidelines are great starting points.
5. Do you offer revisions?
Yes, my process is inherently iterative. We establish feedback loops at key milestones to ensure the direction aligns with your goals.
6. How do I get started?
You can reach out via the contact form in the contact page or email me directly at boopathiuxd@gmail.com to schedule a discovery call.
